Onjula Praise

Born on 14th March in the slums of Gomongo, Nairobi, Onjula Praise grew up surrounded by worship and the rhythms of daily life. As the second-born in a family of three, she is the daughter of the late Dismus Onjula, a pastor who went to be with the Lord in 2022, and Bishop Dolphina Onjula, now a bishop at Sanctuary of Truth Centre.

Being a pastor's kid, she learned how to serve God in all areas, but over time, serving through her voice became her favorite. From singing in church to experimenting with instruments and sound, music became her way of expressing faith, emotions, and storytelling. These early experiences planted the seeds of her passion for worship and creative arts.

influences & vision

Early life

Born in the slums of Gomongo, Nairobi. Her upbringing in a pastor's home taught her obedience, humility, and service from an early age.

Faith & Worship

Her music glorifies God, spreading hope, faith, and inspiration. Every song is a testimony of His grace and love. Inspired by her mother, Bishop Dolphina Onjula.

Music & Creativity

Songwriting begins with an idea, a feeling, or a scripture that moves her. She approaches music as a conversation between heart and spirit.

Community Impact

Serving in church choirs, campus ministries, and local outreach programs has taught her the value of listening, empathy, and encouragement.

Family Foundation

My parents, especially my mother, Bishop Dolphina Onjula, have been a source of inspiration, showing me that faith is not just a belief but a lifestyle expressed through action, love, and dedication.

Worship has always been more than music to me; it is a way to connect with God, inspire others, and live purposefully. This foundation guides every note I sing, every song I write, and every ministry I engage in.
